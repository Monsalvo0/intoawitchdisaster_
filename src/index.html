Welcome to a Witch's Disaster!

Help Brujilda McFizzlepants clean up her spell-tastic chaos! Wield these magical commands to restore order:

  • Press M to charm potions into following your mouse like loyal fireflies.
  • Double-click the bookshelf to neatly arrange those wayward potions—order at last!
  • Click Brujilda’s cauldron to stir up a surprise from its bubbling depths.
  • Press C to reveal the last potion Brujilda was brewing—sneaky, right?
  • Peek at her diary for juicy secrets, but shh—Brujilda McFizzlepants hates nosy readers!
